# Definition
$A=B$ means "for every $x$, $x\in{A}\leftrightarrow{x}\in{B}$" 

$\equiv x\in{A}\to{x}\in{B}\wedge{x}\in{B}\to{x}\in{A}$

Both sets are subsets of each other, i.e: $A\subseteq{B}$ **and** $B\subseteq{A}$
