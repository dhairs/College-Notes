## Project Matrix

The projection of $\left( \frac{fx}{z}, \frac{fy}{z} \right)$ is matrix multiplication.

$$
\begin{bmatrix}
fx \\
fy \\
z
\end{bmatrix} \equiv
\begin{bmatrix}
f & 0 & 0 & 0 \\
0 & f & 0 & 0  \\
0 & 0 & 1 & 0
\end{bmatrix}
\begin{bmatrix}
x \\
y \\
z \\
1
\end{bmatrix}\to
\begin{bmatrix}
\frac{fx}{z} \\
\frac{fy}{z}
\end{bmatrix}
$$
